Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Olla, accessible from the App Store, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Olla and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Olla is a productivity app designed to help you organize your schedule. We collect the schedule data you input (such as task names and times) locally on your device to provide the automation features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate and manage your automated schedule.
  • Send you push notifications for your tasks.
  • Improve and personalize the app experience.

3. Data Storage

Your scheduling data is primarily stored on your device. We do not sell or share your personal scheduling data with third-party companies for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (such as AI processing for schedule generation) to improve our service. These providers only have access to the data necessary to perform their tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.
