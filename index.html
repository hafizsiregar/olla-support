Frequently Asked Questions

How does Olla organize my schedule?

Simply input the name of your activity (e.g., "Futsal"), and our technology will automatically determine the best time slot in your daily schedule.

Can I change a schedule that has already been created?

Of course! You are free to add, edit, or delete any task at any time through our intuitive app interface.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Please ensure that you have granted notification permissions for the Olla app in your device Settings so that reminders can appear on time.
